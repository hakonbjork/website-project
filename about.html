About us

The Martian crew consists of 20 staff members who are happy to help whenever necessary. We have a lot of experience with no-air customer service. The resort consists of a total of three different activities to participate in, along with a nice restaurant with some delicious food. We believe that the atmosphere at Mars creates a unique experience for a family, group of friends, and solo travelers. If you still miss your home planet, we provide “Earth rooms” with artificial plants and birds, to make home seem a little closer.

Our History

The idea of a resort at Mars first appeared in the mind of the famous space traveler Flynn Liardet on a mission to the planet in 2024. Along with fellow astronauts and scientists, he began experiments with fully breathable areas and environments on the planet. A big breakthrough came in 2027, where five of the scientists were able to live, cook, and do daily chores at Mars in over a week. The project received government funding, and a lot of media coverage. In 2032, the first travelers could pay for a maximum a week at the resort. At that time, it was more expensive, and the journey was longer. Over the years, space technology improved. The resort became more affordable, and included several activities, as well as more rooms and possibilities for longer stays. Eventually, it transitioned into the resort as we know it today.
